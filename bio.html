About Me

My name is Molly Risley and I am a fullstack web developer living in Chicago. My love of computers and their scope of customization stems from my middle school indulgence in Sims and Neopets. Since 2006 I've enjoyed the user experience in a plethora of digital novelties. In 2022 I completed Actualize Coding Bootcamp, where I fell in love with the gratification of writing good code and creating a polished, finished product that I can be proud of. I enjoy crushing bugs that felt Goliath, the magic of learning, and seeing it all work!

I previously was an English teacher who taught middle school in Chicago, high school in Brazil, kindergarten in China, and high school in Indiana. I have earned a masters in curriculum development and educational technology. I am passionate about learning management sytems, social emotional learning, and equity in education.

I am an avid crafter and thrifter. My best friends include my 3 tiny dogs. I love to go to local comedy shows and spend time on the lake with friends.
